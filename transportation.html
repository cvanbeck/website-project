A Brief History

Transportation is one of the most important cogs of society. The luxury to go from one place to another on time cannot be underestimated. It all began with the invention of the steamboat by American engineer and inventor Robert Fulton in 1787. As of that time in history, the go-to mode of transport was horse-drawn carts. During following centuries, important transportation inventions would be made in the form of the first bicycle, automobiles, trains and the first aircraft. Fast-forward to the 21st century and thanks to new-age technology, cars can now navigate without human assistance. With the help of Artificial Intelligence (AI) the transportation industry can help reduce the likeliness of road accidents whilst increasing passenger safety, contribute less to global warming with lessen carbon emission and also minimizing financial expenses.

Self Driving Vehicles
A visionary way in which AI has transformed the transportation sector is the creation of self-driving vehicles. A concept that, for a long time, was considered a sci-fi fantasy has quickly become reality. Skepticism was high was the idea was touted and implementation was in its genesis but as of today autonomous vehicles are popular public transport options. Drivers can still be found in these vehicles to take control of them in the sudden event of an emergency. Regardless self-driving vehicles are slowly but steadily becoming a voguish mode of transport whether it is public or private. The concept is still being perfected for safety and to garner more interest but with more innovative tweaks and refinement, autonomous vehicles will become a mainstay mainstream.
Dealing with Traffic Congestion
Another predicament faced on a daily basis in regards to transportation that AI is set to tackle is traffic congestion. A large amount of data on this issue is collected through cameras and sensors which is then sent to cloud storage to be analyzed by an AI-powered system. With the use of big data analytics, traffic patterns can be drawn out to help provide commuters with important information regarding daily transport including predicting traffic and avoiding road blocks and accidents. With the help of this information commuters can be informed about the shortest route to their destination, thus helping them travel without any fuss and cutting time spent on the road significantly.
Helping with Transport Delay
Transport delay is still one of the biggest concerns in the sector, especially air transport. This leads to huge financial loss and mixed negative experience for a passenger. Leveraging AI can help offer the best service for passengers by cutting down wait times and improve overall experience. With the smart use of computer vision, flights can be monitored closely in turn cutting downtime. Other important information like weather and historical records can be processed by machine learning. Realtime number crunching will help reveal hidden patterns that can help the industry unearth useful information on other outcomes that may cause delays and cancellations. With passengers being able to access this data, they can schedule flights accordingly.
In Summary
AI has been one of the most innovative technological advancements so far in history. With all the leaps made so far, we're still yet to scratch the surface of the potential Artificial Intelligence has for humanity. The application of AI in transportation displays the possibilities the technology can offer.
